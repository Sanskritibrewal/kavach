What Actually Happens

Worker Descends Without Testing

No sensor check. No atmospheric test. Entry is routine—based on habit, not data. H₂S and CH₄ accumulate invisibly.

Toxic Gas Makes Contact

H₂S above 50 ppm causes rapid incapacitation. The worker loses consciousness within seconds. There is no warning.

Collapse — Undetected

No check-in timer. No monitoring. Minutes pass before anyone on the surface realises something has gone wrong.

Untrained Rescue Attempts

Co-workers descend without protection to help. They too are overcome. A single fatality becomes a multiple-casualty event.

This Cycle Must Stop.

KAVACH eliminates every failure point—before, during, and after confined space entry—with autonomous AI decision-making.

Protocol

How KAVACH Works

A nine-step autonomous safety protocol from first deployment to final compliance record—requiring zero human judgment for the critical go/no-go decision.

STEP 01
⬇️
Lower Device
KAVACH unit is lowered into the confined space on a rope before any human entry.
STEP 02
🔬
Gas Analysis
Reads H₂S, CH₄, O₂, and CO levels with industrial-grade multi-gas sensors in real time.
STEP 03
🧠
AI Risk Detection
TinyML model on-device runs deterministic safety logic and trend analysis on raw sensor data.
STEP 04
✅
GO / NO-GO Decision
Immediate binary decision displayed on device screen and relayed via BLE to operator's phone.
STEP 05
👷
Controlled Entry
Only on confirmed SAFE signal. Worker identity, timestamp, and clearance are logged automatically.
STEP 06
📡
Live Monitoring
Continuous atmospheric monitoring throughout the work period. Values stream to operator's phone.
STEP 07
⏱️
Check-In Timer
Worker must confirm presence every N minutes via phone. Silence triggers escalation automatically.
STEP 08
🚨
Emergency Escalation
Auto-SMS to supervisor. GPS coordinates. Nearest emergency services notified. Full offline support.
STEP 09
📋
Compliance Record
Immutable digital entry log synced to municipal dashboard. Audit-ready. Timestamped. Signed.

Digital Infrastructure

Built-In Compliance

KAVACH doesn't just protect workers—it creates an unbroken digital chain of accountability from first entry to final audit.
